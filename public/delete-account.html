Delete Your Account

You can delete your Root account and all associated data at any time. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Delete from the app

  1. Open the Root app
  2. Tap Settings in the bottom navigation
  3. Tap Account Settings
  4. Scroll to Danger Zone and tap Delete account
  5. Confirm by tapping Yes, delete

Your account, profile, photos, booking history, and all personal data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Request deletion by email

If you no longer have access to the app, email us and we will delete your account within 7 days:

bmimp1@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your Root account in your message.

What gets deleted: your account credentials, hair profile, photos, booking history, reviews, and messages. This cannot be recovered.
